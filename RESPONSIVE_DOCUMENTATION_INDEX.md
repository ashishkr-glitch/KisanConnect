## ğŸ¯ Common Questions Answered

### **Q: How do I test responsiveness?**
A: Open DevTools (F12), click "Toggle Device Toolbar" (Ctrl+Shift+M), select different devices.

### **Q: What's the minimum screen size supported?**
A: 320px (very old phones). Most phones are 360px+.

### **Q: Can I customize breakpoints?**
A: Yes! Find `@media (max-width: 480px)` and change 480px to your preferred size.

### **Q: Does it work in dark theme?**
A: Yes! All responsive styles work in both light and dark themes.

### **Q: Is there any performance impact?**
A: No! CSS-only responsive design, ~2KB additional CSS.

### **Q: Does it work on all browsers?**
A: Yes! Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge, iOS Safari, Android browsers all supported.

### **Q: How do I test on real devices?**
A: Share your localhost URL (using ngrok or similar) with your phone/tablet.
